WHAT IS PGA?
PIXELS GAME ASSISTANT
PGA is a browser extension that helps Pixels gamers play more efficiently and have more fun. PGA dramatically improves many of the frustrations of playing Pixels game, making them much easier to play and helping you achieve the most in the least amount of time without using a bot.

WHAT FEATURES ARE AVAILABLE?
This is a Preview Beta Version that supports only the following limited features.
SELL ORDER HELPER
When you create a new sell order, it shows the current sell order list and displays the floor price and maximum quantity on the form.
SMART TIMER
You can set timers for various tasks such as VIP lounge, Grumpkin watering, and other custom timers. 
SMART TASKS
You can see the last task board list anywhere.

PGA IS FREE TO USE?
This preview version is completely free of charge.
The upcoming full version may have some premium features requiring paid subscription, and some advanced guild features are available only for participating guild members.
However, even without any paid subscription, all Pixels gamers will find this utility a must to have for enjoying the Pixels game more.
ABOUT GUILDPAL
At GuildPal, we believe that guilds are the best vessel to capture the essence of web3 games. For guilds to thrive, a solid guild platform or protocol that can connect various chains and wallets and offer guilds to build their token model is urgently needed.
We want to describe the goal of this protocol as Guild Abstraction based on Account Abstraction and Chain Abstraction.
With Guild Abstraction, web3 gamers can seamlessly create a decentralized collective community while maintaining full control over their gaming assets. GuildPal will be your pal on your journey to the web3 dream gaming world.
GUILDPAL PIXELS GUILD
We set up a guild in the Pixels game as a showcase. We want to build a strong and helpful community in the Pixels ecosystem and we are open to all individual players and other guilds to work together.
The tools and services we build are not just for our own guild, instead they will serve the broader entire Pixels community.
